Come and experience one of the best places to eat in Norfolk

Nestled in Norfolk’s Wensum Valley, Ringland is about 7 miles north west of Norwich – just off the A1067. It is a picturesque village that is popular with walkers, fishermen and country folk alike. With local beauty spot – the Ringland Hills - just on the doorstop it’s a great place for anyone who enjoys the best of Norfolk’s landscapes.

The Swan is at the heart of the village with lovely views over the River Wensum. So if you’re looking for places to eat in Norfolk, or just want a relaxing pub to enjoy a quiet drink – you couldn’t do better than calling in at The Ringland Swan.
