Our Restaurant

Our light and airy, newly refurbished restaurant combines traditional features like oak beams with modern styling and décor to provide a contemporary, country restaurant. With its glass fronted wine room and graceful Swan chandelier it makes a lovely place to enjoy a leisurely meal.

The restaurant is also available to hire for larger dining parties and functions. We can comfortably accommodate 30-35 people, and the restaurant makes a stylish venue for family celebrations, private functions and business meetings alike. Please get in touch to discuss your requirements or arrange a meeting.
