About us

The Ringland Swan has been fully refurbished and now provides one of the best pubs in Norfolk along with offering a fine dining experience in ourrestaurant.

We’ve retained traditional elements such as the beamed ceiling, exposed brickwork, stone floors and floorboards and combined them with a modern, contemporary design and décor.

The new bar and restaurant both offer comfortable surroundings whatever the weather. The double sided wood burner provides a lovely place to sit when you come in after a walk or on cold winter days.

The cosy welcome is also echoed by hosts Donna and Simon Littleboy and the staff at the Swan. You can always be sure of receiving a warm, friendly welcome whether you are dining in the restaurant or having a drink in the bar.

We have a large garden which makes a lovely backdrop for eating al fresco in the summer.

It’s important to us that all members of the family can enjoy themselves at The Swan. With our new children’s play area The Swan makes a great choice for families looking for child friendly restaurants near Norwich. Children of all ages are welcome and we have a special menu just for them. We also have great baby changing facilities.

Dogs are welcome in the bar area. So if you’re looking for a dog friendly pub in Norfolk, The Swan is an ideal choice and means you don’t have to leave your four legged family members at home.

We have full disabled access and facilities.

The Swan has a large off-road car park.
