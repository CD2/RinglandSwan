Excellent dining experience

Our chefs are serious about providing excellent food and drink to our customers whether you are dining in our restaurant or eating in the bar area. We pride ourselves on providing the best quality gastro style menu using the best local produce.

Our suppliers include:

Toombes Butchers

Toombes Butchers supply all our meat and have over 30 years’ experience in sourcing the best local meat. From Norfolk farms to Norfolk plates.

Easters (Norwich) Ltd

They have been supplying good quality fruit and veg for over 35 years.

We cook all our steaks and burgers on a char pit, giving them that fabulous flavour that only cooking on an open fire gives.

We vary our menus regularly and source local ingredients whenever we can. We will always try to provide personal menus for customers booking private functions when needed.

We also have an extensive range of local Real Ales, craft beers and fine wines for you to choose from.
